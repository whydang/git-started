Finding Nemo

Nemo is a little clownfish who lost his mother to a hungry fish. His dad is overprotective and as a result, when the school went on a trip to the drop-off, he swam up to touch the butt, which really is just a boat. A diver happened to swim by and captured him for his little niece as a pet. In the aquarium he was placed in, he met some new friends who in the end helped him escape to find his dad. In the end, he reunited with his dad, yay!



Nemo is in the Disney movie Finding Nemo which released in 2003. During the time of release, it was a very popular film that won the hearts of both children and adults. It teaches everyone the the true meaning of family and the importance of trust.
